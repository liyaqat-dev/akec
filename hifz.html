Khaja Mueenudheen Hifzul Qur'an Academy

Spiritual Excellence through Qur'anic Memorization

Academic Aim

The aim is to identify students who are capable of memorizing the Holy Qur'an and to enable them to complete Hifz by memorizing the Qur'an according to Tajweed. We provide the opportunity for students who have passed the fifth grade to complete Hifz and also provide the necessary training to pass the tenth grade. Along with this, the syllabus has been designed with an emphasis on spiritual lifestyle training and personality development.

Admission Details

  • Admission Entry After 5th Standard
  • Selection Process Interview Based
  • Academic Stream Regular Schooling & Hifz
  • Focus Areas Tajweed, Spiritual Training, Personality Development

Key Features

  • Expert Tajweed Instructors
  • Spiritual Mentoring
  • 10th Grade Board Support
  • Residential Facilities

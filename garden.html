Academic Vision

The Mueeniyya Garden of Qur'an Studies for Girls offers an integrated educational framework designed to empower female students with both spiritual wisdom and academic proficiency. Students who have successfully completed their 5th Standard are eligible to join this institution. We provide a specialized curriculum that focuses on the memorization and study of the Holy Qur'an alongside regular Islamic studies. Parallel to their religious education, students are guided and supported to complete their SSLC (10th Standard) with high academic standards, ensuring they are well-prepared for both their spiritual journeys and future professional careers.

Integrated Curriculum

  • Qur'an Memorization (Hifz)
  • Islamic Jurisprudence (Fiqh)
  • Arabic Language Studies

Academic Goals

  • SSLC Support & Coaching
  • Personality Development
  • Moral Values Training

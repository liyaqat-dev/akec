Our Legacy

Decades of Educational & Social Commitment

Ajmeer Khaja Educational Complex (AKEC), situated in Desamangalam, Thrissur, stands as a beacon of hope and excellence. For decades, we have been dedicated to the holistic development of society through value-based education and extensive humanitarian activities.

Our institutions are designed to nurture students from various backgrounds, providing them with the tools to excel in both their professional careers and their spiritual lives. We believe that true education is the one that builds character alongside competence.

Our Vision

Our Mission

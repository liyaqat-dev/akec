About the Institution

This system, designed with the aim of not deviating from the moral life in the chaotic flow of society and to form an excellent religious teaching cadre, is affiliated with Jami'atul Hind Al Islamiyya under Samastha Kerala Jam-iyyathul Ulama. Along with Islamic education, it provides free education in Islamic Jurisprudence, Arabic Grammar, Language Studies, and Computer Studies such as Digital Design, Accounting, and Web Design, up to postgraduate degrees. Students have been studying in courses such as PG, Engineering, Chartered Accounting, Airport Management, and Logistics. Al Mueini graduates who have graduated from the institutions work in various parts of the country in the fields of teaching and service.
